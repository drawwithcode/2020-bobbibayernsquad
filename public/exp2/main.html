HOW DOES IT WORK?

Imagine you're standing in front of your desk. It's completely dark and you need to find a bell, placed somewhere on it.

So you start patting the surface to sense the different objects that your hands can reach: their shape, the sound they make... until you know that you found what you were looking for.

It's not impossible, you just have to look without using your eyes!
